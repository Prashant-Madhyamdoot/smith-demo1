About

A media researcher between industry and the academy.

Smith holds a PhD in Creative Industries (2021) from the Queensland University of Technology, Australia. His primary focus lies on the digital transformation of the screen industries and its impact on the power dynamics in creative labour and production.

Before academia, he spent nearly a decade inside the industry — working at Viacom18 Media (Bollywood) as an associate producer across nine feature films and a web-series, and was selected as a UNESCO Asia-Pacific Creative Specialist in 2018–19. That dual vantage point — practitioner and scholar — runs through all of his work.
